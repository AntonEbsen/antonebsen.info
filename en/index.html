Biography

Short (3rd person)

Anton Meier Ebsen Jørgensen is an economist (M.Sc. Econ.) specializing in macroeconomic modeling and monetary policy. He combines quantitative analysis with the communication of complex economic trends.

Extended (3rd person)

Anton Meier Ebsen Jørgensen holds a Master's degree in Economics (cand.polit.) from the University of Copenhagen. His academic focus lies at the intersection of econometrics and international macroeconomics, with a particular interest in global financial cycles and the spillover effects of US monetary policy on the global economy.

In his professional work, he deals with advanced economic models and data analysis, utilizing tools such as Python, SAS, and GAMS to illuminate complex issues. In addition to technical work, he is an active voice in economic debate through lectures and written contributions, focusing on making economic theory accessible and relevant to a broader audience.
